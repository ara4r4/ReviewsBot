Reviews Bot - Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Reviews Bot collects, uses, and secures your data when you use our services. By inviting and using Reviews Bot, you agree to the data collection practices described in this policy.

1. Data We Collect

Reviews Bot only collects publicly available data, including but not limited to:

  • Usernames
  • Guild (server) names
  • Suggestions submitted by users

No private or sensitive personal data is collected. The bot does not have access to private messages, user profile data, or any other information beyond what is publicly viewable.

2. Data Storage and Retention

All data collected by Reviews Bot is stored securely on cloud servers. The bot stores suggestions for up to one month before permanently clearing them. Data is kept as minimal as possible to ensure efficient bot functionality while maintaining user privacy.

3. Data Usage

Data collected by Reviews Bot is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing insights to improve the functionality of the bot
  • Maintaining accurate operation within your Discord server
  • Facilitating the submission and management of user suggestions

We do not share, sell, or otherwise disclose collected data to third parties under any circumstances.

4. User Consent

By inviting Reviews Bot to your server, you consent to the collection and use of publicly available data as described in this policy. Users can withdraw their consent by removing the bot from their server or requesting the deletion of stored data.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of their data at any time. To request data deletion, users can contact us via the support server.

6. Security Measures

We take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted and protected by industry-standard security measures, including IP protection and two-factor authentication (2FA). Access to stored data is limited to authorized personnel only.

7. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as necessary to comply with legal requirements and changes in our data practices. Any updates will be announced in our support server.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us through our support server.
